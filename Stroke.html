Stroke

Damage to the brain because it was exposed to blood

Three Main Types of Strokes

  1. Ischemic stroke
  2. Hemorrhagic stroke
  3. Transient ischemic attack (a warning or “mini-stroke”)

What Causes Strokes

  • Ischemic Stroke is caused by a blocked artery
  • Hemorrhagic stroke is caused by a leaking or bursting of a blood vessel
  • Transient ischemic attack is a temporary disruption of blood flow to the brain which doesn't cause lasting symptoms

Symptoms

  • Trouble walking
  • Trouble speaking
  • Trouble understanding

Treatment

If the stroke is treated immediately it can minimize brain damage. It can be treated by Medication (tPA-clot buster)or by Surgery.

Symptoms that may occur after Stroke

Paralisis, may affect vision and speech, weakens muscles, reduces sensation of touch, difficulty balancing, headaches, inability to understand, and confusion.
